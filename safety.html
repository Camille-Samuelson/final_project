Children, elders, pets–as a care provider, you put their safety first. We do too.

Tribe vets each caretaker, conducting criminal background checks and sex offender registry searches, and takes into account reviews from verified users, because there’s physical safety, and then there’s also psychological safety and comfort.

Safety isn’t just in who is part of the tribe, but how we care for one another. Care providers put infants on their backs to sleep. They ensure children use safety equipment like helmets when riding scooters or bicycles.
